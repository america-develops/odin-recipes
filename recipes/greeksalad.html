Recipe

Ingredients

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1 cup olive oil (can be reduced to 3/4 cup)
  • 1 cup olive oil (can be reduced to three fourths cup)
  • Juice of 1 extra large lemon (at room temperature)
  • 2 to 4 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 3 teaspoons minced garlic (fresh)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • One half teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper (or to taste)
  • One half teaspoon fresh ground black pepper (or to taste)

Salad Ingredients

  • 1 large head romain lettuce, chopped
  • 2 to 3 plum tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 English cucumber (peeled, seeded, and chopped)
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 pound crumbled feta cheese (or to taste)
  • One half pound crumbled feta cheese (or to taste)
  • 1 cup kalamata olives

Instructions

Dressing Instructions

  1. Combine the lemon juice, dried oregano, garlic, dried basil, red wine vinegar, salt and sugar.
  2. Using a food processor or whisk, blend the olive oil into the lemon juice mixture until smooth.
  3. Season with the black pepper.
  4. Chill in the fridge for a couple of hours so the flavors can blend.

Salad Instructions

  1. Place all the salad ingredients into a large bowl.
  2. Pour dressing over the ingredients and then toss to combine.
  3. If desired, allow the salad to sit at room temperature for 20 minutes before serving. (Optionally, you can refrigerate the salad for 1 hour before serving.)
